Artist Statement

Five years ago I created my first logo. It has been the symbol behind all my work in cinematography. Its a play off a childhood nickname and has evolved into something much more than a symbol. This 3-D model was built upon the original design and adapted into an island. For years I've been hoping to do this project and update the logo design, and now that it is complete, will further evolve into something greater!
