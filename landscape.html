Artist Statement

This is a continuation of the Remix Montage. Animating different layers from the photo to convey a new generation escaping old ways. With the combination of old and new technology, we take from our past and develop the future. While the future is unknown, and can be quite scary to some, leaving our past to shape the future becomes a distant idea, with unimaginable beauty.
